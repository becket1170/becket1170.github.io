Philosophy

I teach undergraduate and graduate courses in English literature at the University of Saskatchewan, with a focus on medieval literature, the history of the English language, and manuscript culture. My teaching draws on primary texts in Middle English and seeks to give students direct experience with the interpretive challenges posed by manuscript evidence.
